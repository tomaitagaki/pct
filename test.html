Probabilistic Categorization Task

Instructions

In this task, you will see different shapes (▲, ■, ●) appear on the screen.

Your goal is to learn which category (A or L) each shape belongs to.

Press 'A' or 'L' on your keyboard to make your choice.

After each response, you'll receive feedback about whether you were correct.

Note that the same shape might belong to different categories at different times - try to learn the patterns!
